Summary

I have two passions: optimising the operational flow of a business, and high performance sport. My path has run through both. I led basketball high performance and operations at AUSA Hoops, and worked in sports science at the Sydney Kings, GWS Giants and Penrith Panthers, always in small teams where efficiency and data-driven decisions did the heavy lifting. At AIM, a running sunglasses brand, I brought the same lens to e-commerce operations. I love small business, which is why I founded my own agency, Subject:RAW, and why I build the agentic workflows, dashboards and tools that help small teams move faster, working across Python, FastAPI, Next.js, Postgres and Claude.
